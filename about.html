About Us

The Faythe Marie Brannon Foundation was established in 2024 in honor of the incomparable Faythe Marie Brannon. Ms. Brannon dedicated her life to education and empowering youth. She was a light that shined brightly to all who knew her and her passion for the youth transformed numerous lives. The Foundation strives to support and enrich the lives of youth via educational and financial resources, as well as the social-emotional skills they need to succeed. Your generous support will ensure success for generations of youth!
