# The Faythe Marie Brannon Foundation

> *"You don't go where the path may lead you. Go where there is no path and leave a trail."* – Faythe Marie Brannon

A memorial foundation website dedicated to honoring the legacy of Ms. Faythe Marie Brannon through education, empowerment, and community support.

## 🌟 About

The Faythe Marie Brannon Foundation was established to continue the mission of Ms. Faythe Marie Brannon, an extraordinary educator and technology pioneer who dedicated her life to supporting, uplifting, and empowering students in Chicago public schools. After her unexpected passing on September 10, 2023, this foundation carries forward her vision of empowering youth through education and community support.

### Ms. Faythe's Legacy
- **Education**: Earned a bachelor's degree from Southern Illinois University and two master's degrees in education and computer science
- **Leadership**: Served as President of the CSTA (Computer Science Teachers Association) Chapter in Chicago
- **Innovation**: Pioneer who led the Bowen High School Robotics Team and hosted robotics tournaments for inner city schools
- **Recognition**: Led Bowen High School to receive the 2022 College Board AP Computer Science Female Diversity Award
- **Community**: Active member of the National Council of Negro Women Chicago Central Section

## 🎓 Mission

Our foundation continues Faythe's work by:
- **Awarding Scholarships**: Providing $1,000 scholarships to graduating seniors at Bowen High School and Harlan High School
- **Empowering Youth**: Supporting underrepresented students in technology and academia
- **Building Community**: Creating opportunities for growth and learning
- **Honoring Legacy**: Keeping Faythe's spirit of empowerment alive

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Web browser
- Text editor (optional, for modifications)

### Local Development
1. Clone the repository:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/izaher18/FaytheFoundation.git
   cd FaytheFoundation
   ```

2. Open `index.html` in your web browser, or serve with a local server:
   ```bash
   # Using Python 3
   python -m http.server 8000
   
   # Using Node.js (if you have http-server installed)
   npx http-server
   ```

3. Navigate to `http://localhost:8000` to view the website

### Deployment
The site is configured for deployment on Vercel. Simply connect your GitHub repository to Vercel for automatic deployments.
